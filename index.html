Who We Are

SheSolar Innovation & Technology is more than an organization — it is a platform, a community, and a network dedicated to empowering women in solar energy, renewable energy, and technology.

Founded on real industry experience, SheSolar exists to bridge the gender gap in technical expertise, leadership representation, and business opportunities within the clean energy ecosystem.

What We Do

  • Skills Development & Training
  • Community & Networking
  • Business & Career Support
  • Advocacy & Innovation

Why It Matters

The global transition to clean energy presents enormous opportunities — yet women remain underrepresented in technical and leadership roles.

About SheSolar

SheSolar Innovation & Technology is a women-led initiative dedicated to empowering African women through solar energy, renewable energy, technology, and innovation.

Our Vision

A future where women actively lead in renewable energy, technology innovation, and sustainable development — building businesses, creating jobs, influencing policy, and shaping the global energy landscape.

Our Mission

To empower women with the skills, knowledge, support systems, and opportunities needed to succeed and lead in solar energy, renewable energy, and clean technology.

Join Our Vision

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: Equipping women with practical tools for success.
  • Integrity: Operating with professionalism, transparency, and excellence.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creative solutions and forward thinking.
  • Sisterhood: Building strong networks of collaboration and support.
  • Impact: Driving meaningful social, economic, and environmental change.
